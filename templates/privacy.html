Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the app, we store the expense records you create — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information or any data beyond what you explicitly enter.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and enabling filtering. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app on your behalf.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on our servers and is associated with your account. We take reasonable precautions to protect it, including hashing passwords before storage. However, as a portfolio project, Spendly is not intended to store sensitive financial data and should not be used as a primary financial record.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces. This means a request is made to Google's servers when you visit the site. We do not integrate with any analytics platforms, advertising networks, or external data processors.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or would like to request deletion of your data, please reach out at arora.rishabh007@gmail.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
